Lasagna recipe

This is a pizza -.-

There is a saying in Italy: "A pizza a day keeps the doctor away"

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Ready dough :)

Step by step

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Prepare the tomato puree and spread it over the dough
  3. Cut the mozzarella and toss it over
  4. Bake for 2 seconds at 600 degrees
